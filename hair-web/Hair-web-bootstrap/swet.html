What is Regenerative Therapy?

Regenerative thinning hair treatments go to the source — the follicles — to rehabilitate them, making them healthier and more productive. The hair follicle is one of the very few structures in the body that can resume healthy functioning after having shut down. That is the goal of our nature hair regrowth treatments. We help your hair follicles wake up again,re-enter the anagen stage, and begin growing healthy new hair.
